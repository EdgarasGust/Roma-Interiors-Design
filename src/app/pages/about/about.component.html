About me

I am a practical modernist interior designer. My passion stems from my lifelong love of architecture, interiors and travel and I get my inspiration from the countries I`ve visited combined with my love of nature. I am also a self confessed homebody which helps me to understand my clients need to want to relax and chill out in their homes.

Originally from Lithuania, I`ve lived in London for over 20 years. I began after moving into my first flat which was an empty shell and I didn't know how to turn it into a home. So I started reading books, magazines and watching “Grand Designs”. After quite a bit of trial and error, I fell in love with the process and became an Interior Designer.

I`ve created many different and varied looks but my personal style leans towards a modern contemporary style fused with rustic and industrial touches. I love to include artisanal & hand-made pieces into the interiors I design. I am also a wife and mother of 2, which helps me keep it real and inspires me to create spaces that are not only aesthetically interesting but also stylishly liveable with a strong practical element.

I believe in a collaborative approach and work closely with my clients to understand their practical needs, mixing this with decor styles and ideas they love to create a real `wow` factor within any given space - from one room to an entire house

Designs for Casual Everyday Living with just a hint of Wow!
