How it works

We offer a simple three-step process, where you will get exactly what you have always wanted. We will provide you with an easy and affordable design service. And everything will be done at the perfect pace for you without ever having to worry about what it will cost. Everything is included. It allows you to budget ahead with complete clarity and peace of mind.

It is the most popular service we have ever offered.

When you choose the package, you are redirected to a comprehensive questionnaire to complete that will help you clarify your design needs and what inspires your style and preferences. As part of the process we will also gather: The floor plans and measurements of the rooms to be designed Photos from each corner of every room Photos of items that you would particularly wish to keep and their measurements (if not replacing all furniture) Here is the simple guide: how to measure your space Measurements guidance Once we have received all of this information, we will confirm the timescales and send you an email with payment details. After payment, our professional design team will start the creative process taking into account your wishes and preferences given in the questionnaire. We send the completed conceptual interior design to you including a detailed shopping list with links, and useful tips on how to bring your new design alive in your home. Where you have chosen Standard or Exclusive packages, you may request a revision at this point.
